FROG TYPING GAME

Welcome to the frog typing game!
In this game you will play as this frog
to practice speed at typing in English.


Feed the frog to win the game!


HOW TO PLAY

This game has simple controls. Type
the words that appear on the screen to
eat the insects.


On levels B and C you can also move
your mouse left and right to avoid
being hit.


Make sure to not get hit or you'll die, and
be careful with the bigger ones: they will
summon new enemies to attack you!


Each level will have waves that you will
have to survive to continue playing.
